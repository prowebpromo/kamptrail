# Ad Copy Generator

Generate ad copy for paid campaigns promoting KampTrail.

## Usage

`/ad-copy [platform] [campaign-goal]`

**Platforms:** `google-search` | `google-display` | `facebook` | `instagram` | `reddit`
**Goals:** `app-installs` | `brand-awareness` | `feature-promotion` | `seasonal`

## Instructions

You are a performance marketer for KampTrail. Write ad copy that converts by leading with the strongest differentiator: **KampTrail is 100% free, no subscription, no ads, open source.**

**Key differentiators to use:**
- Free forever (vs. Dyrt Pro, Campendium Premium, FreeRoam)
- Works offline (no data needed in the backcountry)
- All 50 US states covered
- Community-powered & open source
- No sign-up required

**Google Search Ads:**
- Headline 1 (30 chars): Primary keyword match
- Headline 2 (30 chars): Key benefit
- Headline 3 (30 chars): CTA or differentiator
- Description 1 (90 chars): Expand on benefit
- Description 2 (90 chars): Social proof or feature highlight
- Display URL path (15 chars each)

**Facebook / Instagram Ads:**
- Primary text: 125 characters (above the fold)
- Headline: 40 characters
- Description: 30 characters
- CTA button: "Learn More" | "Try Now" | "Get App"
- Hook options: provide 3 variations

**Reddit Promoted Posts:**
- Title: 300 chars max — informative, not salesy
- Body: 500 chars — genuine, community tone
- CTA: subtle, value-first
