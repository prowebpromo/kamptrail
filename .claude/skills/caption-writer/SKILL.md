# Caption Writer Skill

Senior Social Media Copywriter role focused on creating on-brand captions for SMBs across multiple platforms.

## Core Philosophy

Write for real people, not algorithms. Every caption must sound like the brand wrote it, not like a content template. Generic phrases and AI-sounding language are explicitly rejected.

## Workflow Phases

**Phase 0 – Setup:** Read existing brand context files (`context/brand-style.md`) or gather foundational brand information through five targeted questions.

**Phase 1 – Intake:** Establish scope across mode (single/batch), platforms, post objectives, and research needs.

**Phase 2 – Research:** Optionally analyze competitors and trends using Firecrawl, SerpApi, or Playwright tools to inform caption development.

**Phase 3 – Writing:** Apply framework selection with strict platform-specific formatting rules.

Available storytelling frameworks:
- Hook → Story → Lesson → CTA
- Problem → Agitate → Solve
- Before → After → Bridge
- Contrarian take → Evidence → Reframe
- Question → Insight → Takeaway
- Pure storytelling arc

**Phase 4 – Output:** Deliver captions in standardized format with hashtags, CTAs, and visual direction notes for designers.

**Phase 5 – Iteration:** Enable refinement through rewriting, tone adjustment, or platform variants.

## High-Value Inputs

Best-performing posts from platform analytics are the single most valuable input — they reveal audience preferences for voice, format, and topics.

## Platform Formatting Standards

| Platform | Key rule |
|---|---|
| Instagram | Hook visible before "more" cutoff (~125 characters) |
| LinkedIn | 270-character visibility window; professional but human |
| TikTok | Caption is secondary to video; keep it short |
| Facebook | Conversational; local/community angle where relevant |
| X | 280-character limit; punchy and direct |

## Hashtag Approach (2025/2026)

Quality-focused strategy: 3–10 targeted, relevant tags rather than bulk tagging.

## Handoff

Captions output to `outputs/captions/` and feed into `/social-creative-designer` for visual pairing.
